Bacon Cheddar Chive Scones

Description

This recipe comes from the King Arthur Baking website. I have made it a number of times and it is delicious!

Who doesn't love bacon? Adding in some sharp cheddar, and the chive (or green onion) flavor, makes for a delicous, flaky savory treat!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups All Purpose or Pastry Flour
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp baking poweder
  • 2 tsp sugar
  • 4 tbsp cold butter
  • 1 cup coarsely grated or diced cheddar cheese
  • 1/3 cup snipped fresh chives or finely diced scallion tops (the green part only)
  • 1/2 lb. bacon- cooked, cooled & crumbled
  • 3/4 + 2 tbsp heavy or whipping cream, or enough to make the dough cohesive
